Mac N Cheese

Description

A simple but amazing classic, mac n cheese is sure to be a crowd pleaser. Excellent for any occasion!

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 pounds whole-wheat macaroni
  • 3/4 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 6 cups milk, divided
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon mustard powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 8 ounce shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
  • 8 ounce shredded American cheese
  • 8 ounce potato chips, crushed
  • 1 cup shredded Chedar cheese
  • 1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • butter-flavored cooking spray
