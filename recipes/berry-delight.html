Berried Delight

Description

Berried Delight is a delicious desert that doesn't take too long to prepare. Enjoy strawberries embeded into Jello on top of some graham crackers topped with whipped cream.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups HONEY MAID Graham Cracker Crumbs
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 6 tablespoons butter or margarine, melted
  • 8 oz. Cream Cheese, softened
  • 2 tablespoons cold milk
  • 8 oz. Cool Whip whipped Topping, thawed, divided
  • 4 cups strawberries, hulled, halved
  • 3 1/2 cups cold milk
  • 2 pkg. JELL-O Vanilla Flavor Instant Pudding

  • 2 pkg. JELL-O Vanilla Flavor Instant Pudding

Steps

  1. Mix crumbs, sugar, and butter in 13x9 inch pan; press onto bottom of pan.
  2. Refrigerate 15 minutes.
  3. Beat cream cheese and 2 tablespoons of milk in a large bowl with wire whisk until well blended.
  4. Gently stir in half of the whipped topping.
  5. Spread onto curst; top with strawberries.
  6. Pour 3 1/2 cups of milk into large bowl.
  7. Add dry pudding mixes. Beat 2 minutes
  8. Pour over strawberries.
  9. Refrigerate for 4 hours or until set.
  10. Spread with remaining whipped topping just before serving.
